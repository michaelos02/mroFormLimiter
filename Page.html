How to Use Form Limiter

Form Limiter automatically closes your Google Form when specific conditions are met.

Available Options:

  • Response Limit: Close after receiving a specific number of responses (1-10,000)
  • Date & Time Limit: Close at a specific date and time
  • Combined Limits: Use both - the form closes when the first condition is met

How It Works:

  • Set your desired limits in the Settings tab
  • Click "Save Settings" to activate
  • The form automatically stops accepting responses when any limit is reached
  • All triggers are cleaned up when the form closes

Tips:

  • You can modify limits anytime by reopening this dialog
  • Date without time defaults to 11:59 PM
  • Response limits take effect immediately when reached
  • Times use your local timezone
